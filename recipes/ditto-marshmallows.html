Ditto Marshmallows

We hope these marshmallows don't change shape on us!

Ingredients

  • 2 egg whites
  • 20g sugar
  • Pink food coloring, as needed
  • Light blue food coloring, as needed
  • Chocolate decorating pen, as needed

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add egg whites and beat until foamy.
  2. Add the sugar in two parts, and beat until stiff peaks form.
  3. Mix in pink and light blue food coloring and mix.
  4. Add in the Gelatin and mix. Transfer the mixture to a pastry bag.
  5. On a tray dusted with potato starch, pipe the gelatin mixture in the shape of Ditto or any shape you desire.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator until set.
  7. Draw in the faces with chocolate decorating pens. Enjoy!
  8. That's it
